Quote tips

Photos make estimates faster and clearer.

For most projects, the fastest path is to send your city, photos of the area, product links or model numbers, measurements if you have them, and your ideal timeline.

Helpful details to include

  • Your city or neighborhood
  • Photos of the project area
  • Product links, instructions, or model numbers
  • Measurements, TV size, or wall type if known
  • Whether you already have parts or hardware
  • Preferred timing and contact method
