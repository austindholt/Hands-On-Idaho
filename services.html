What I do

Handyman services for practical home projects.

From quick repairs to custom problem solving, Hands-On Idaho helps Treasure Valley homeowners finish the projects that are too small, too weird, or too time-consuming to keep ignoring.

Repairs & Punch-List Items

Good for the little things that stack up around the house.

  • Door, latch, hinge, and hardware adjustments
  • Trim, caulk, drywall, paint touch-up coordination
  • Cabinet pulls, towel bars, hooks, and small installs
  • General troubleshooting and practical repair planning

Mounting, Assembly & Installs

Clean installs that feel finished, level, and secure.

  • TV mounting and basic cable cleanup
  • Shelving, curtain rods, mirrors, and wall-mounted items
  • Furniture assembly and garage storage
  • Fixture and hardware swaps where appropriate

Maintenance & Exterior Help

Keep the outside of the house from becoming a bigger problem later.

  • Yard cleanup and small exterior projects
  • Seasonal home walk-throughs
  • Gate, fence, garage, and exterior hardware checks
  • Basic preventative maintenance recommendations

Tech-Friendly Home Upgrades

Modern home setup help without making it more complicated than it needs to be.

  • Smart doorbells, smart switches, and smart-home setup
  • Wi-Fi/mesh network setup support
  • Home audio, TV, and device setup
  • Smart doorbell, camera, and customer-provided device setup as homeowner support

Custom Projects

Some jobs do not fit neatly into one category — that’s usually where I can help.

  • Small custom builds and practical modifications
  • Problem-solving around awkward spaces or old installs
  • Homeowner project support and second opinions
  • “Can you take a look at this?” type jobs

Not Sure Where It Fits?

Send a message with photos and a short description. If it’s a good fit, I’ll help you plan it. If it needs a licensed specialist or a bigger contractor, I’ll be honest about that.
