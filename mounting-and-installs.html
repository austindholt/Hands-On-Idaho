FAQ

Mounting questions.

Can you install customer-provided products?

Yes, most projects start with customer-provided shelves, rods, storage kits, mirrors, or fixtures.

Can heavy items be mounted?

Often, but the wall, hardware, weight, and intended use matter. Photos and product details help confirm the plan.

Do you handle tile or masonry?

Some surfaces may be possible, but they need extra care. Send photos so I can review before booking.
