FAQ

Do you assemble flat-pack furniture?

Yes. Send the product link or model so I can understand size and complexity.

Often, depending on the product, wall surface, and included hardware. Send photos so I can review.

Do I need to unbox everything first?

Not always, but having the boxes in the room and enough workspace helps the job go smoothly.
