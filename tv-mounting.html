FAQ

TV mounting questions.

Do I need to already have a mount?

It helps, but if you are unsure what to buy, send the TV model, wall type, and desired placement and I can help you think through options.

Can wires be hidden?

Many cords can be cleaned up or concealed neatly. Any in-wall power or electrical changes should be handled carefully and may require a licensed electrician.

What areas do you serve?

Common service areas include Boise, Meridian, Eagle, Nampa, Kuna, Star, Garden City, and nearby Treasure Valley neighborhoods.

Can you mount a soundbar too?

Yes, soundbar mounting and basic home theater setup are common add-ons for TV mounting projects.
